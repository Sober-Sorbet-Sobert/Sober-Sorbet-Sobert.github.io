About Dolphins

Dolphins are marine mammals known for their intelligence, playful behavior, and friendly nature. They live in oceans and some rivers around the world.

Fun Dolphin Facts

  • Dolphins use echolocation to find food and navigate.
  • They can swim up to 25 miles per hour.
  • Dolphins live in groups called pods.
  • Some dolphins can hold their breath for over 10 minutes.
